about

The purpose of this site is to record various events that occur in daily life. From facts to paranormal phenomena, we record daily events in this world. The main object of observation is the world of subculture. I'm not going to do that complicated thing. Everyday life is nearing the end. Fortunately, I can rely on the power of HUGO + ananke when my daily memories are likely to become ambiguous.
